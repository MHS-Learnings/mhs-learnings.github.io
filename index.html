About The Olympiad

اولمپیاڈ کا تعارف

MHS Learnings presents the National Mathematics Olympiad 2026. This prestigious competition is designed to challenge, inspire, and identify the brightest mathematical minds across the nation's top schools. It provides a competitive platform for students to showcase their problem-solving skills at a national level.
